About Nakshetra

Message from the Director

A word from our leadership

Director

Manim Parajuli

Director

Nakshetra Education & Visa Services

Dear Students and Parents,

Deciding to study abroad is a big step, and I truly understand how important this decision is for both students and families. It's not just about choosing a country or a course - it's about your future, your dreams, and the life you want to build.

Every student has different goals, strengths, and circumstances. That's why at Nakshatra Education and Visa Services, we focus on understanding you first. We listen to your plans, your concerns, and your expectations before guiding you toward the right options. Our aim is to make the entire process simple, clear, and stress-free - from selecting the right program to handling applications and visa procedures.

We believe in giving honest advice and practical support. Sometimes the best guidance is not about choosing the most expensive option, but the most suitable one. As a team, we are committed to standing by you at every stage and helping you move forward with confidence.

Your success is personal to us, and we are here to support you in turning your aspirations into reality.
